---
title: There's no such thing as an AI product
date: 2026-04-08
tag: product
featured: true
---

AI is a technology. Like Java. You don't call something a Java product — you call it what it actually does for the person using it.

The term "AI product" has always made me slightly uncomfortable. Not because AI isn't real or important, but because naming it that way puts the technology at the center instead of the person. And in my experience, that's exactly backwards from how good products get built.

When I was building computer vision products at VIP.com in 2014, nobody called them "AI products." We called them "visual search" and "smart recommendations" — because that's what they did for users. The AI was the engine, invisible under the hood.

The best products I've shipped have this in common: users never think about how they work. They just feel like magic.

That's the job. Make the technology disappear.
