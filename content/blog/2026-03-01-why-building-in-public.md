date: 2026-03-01

I'm better at writing than talking. Always have been.

In meetings I often have the thought two minutes after I should have said it. In writing I can take the thought apart, see if it holds, put it back together in the right order.

So I'm going to write more. About what I'm building, what I'm learning, what I think is wrong about how we talk about AI products. Some of it will be polished. Most of it will be rough. That's fine.

The point isn't to have all the answers. The point is to think out loud in a place where the thinking accumulates.

If something here resonates — I'd genuinely love to hear from you.
